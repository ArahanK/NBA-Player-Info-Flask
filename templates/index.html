NBA Player Visualizer

The idea for the NBA-visualizer came to me during my first NBA Fantasy Draft. During the drafting of players, I really hoped that I could see their statistics listed out over a period of time, and determine how likely they were to do well. From there, I got the idea to create a visualizer of my own, properly displaying all the aspects of an athlete's gameplay so that I could be more informed in the world of sports.

This website was built using the Flask framework for front-end, and several APIs, NumPy, and Pandas for the backend.
